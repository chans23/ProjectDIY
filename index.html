Publishing to Me

Growing up with limited access to publications that I liked, owning a copy of my favorite manga, translated novel, or artbook is something special. Buying a physical copy means that I am supporting the creators and shows that there are people willing to spend money on this kind of genre. This means that there will be more investment in the genre.

My appreciation for a published work goes as far as buying a copy in the original language or even in a different language!

The idea of having a physical copy to prove that I am a published author manifests and is evident in this photo. Although digital publications cost less, the ability to share paid content is very easy because of the web. To own a printed copy lets me know that I am directly supporting creators.
